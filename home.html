CONCEPT

01. Move

Our applications make it easy to move! With our services, local residents gain speed for their journeys, public transport or even carpooling.

02. Accumulate

Our concept is simple: by moving with our apps, users earn points. The more points they earn, the more they earn rewards!

03. Earn !

In addition to earning points, we reward travel. Do you want a discount on your next restaurant ticket? So travel, accumulate and win!

About

TransWay publishes solutions in order to help create smart cities of tomorrow. Designed for transport networks, communities, and enterprises, TransWay's offers improve the quality of urban services while reducing costs. Our offers allow a statistical evaluation of the data recovered from our own tools, as well as external services (ticketing, mobile phones, GPS...). TransWay also provides assistance in the fields of mobility and technology related to passenger transportation.
